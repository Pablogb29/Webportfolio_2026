# Definition Of Done

## Content

- Positioning is immediately understandable.
- No fake, inflated, or weakly supported claims remain.
- Dates, titles, and terminology are consistent.
- Each section has a clear purpose and supports the overall narrative.

## UX And Design

- Visual hierarchy is strong on desktop and mobile.
- The interface feels premium, restrained, and cybersecurity-native.
- Navigation is clear and CTAs are obvious.
- No visual clutter or decorative noise remains.

## Engineering

- Components are maintainable and reusable.
- No unnecessary dependencies were introduced.
- Motion respects reduced-motion preferences.
- The site performs well and avoids layout instability.

## Accessibility

- Semantic structure is correct.
- Contrast is acceptable.
- Keyboard and focus behavior are usable.
- Touch targets and mobile spacing are adequate.

## SEO

- Every route has intentional metadata.
- Headings and internal links support discoverability.
- Structured data is added where relevant.

## Hiring Impact

- A recruiter can identify role fit quickly.
- A technical reviewer can find proof without digging.
- The portfolio feels differentiated from generic developer portfolios.
