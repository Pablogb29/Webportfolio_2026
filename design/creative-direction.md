# Creative Direction

## Aesthetic Target

Operator-grade calm. The site should feel precise, expensive, and technically mature.

## Inspiration Zones

- Premium security consultancy
- Senior engineering portfolio
- Modern technical product design
- Understated luxury interfaces

## Visual Do

- Use a dark neutral base with a restrained accent system.
- Let typography and spacing carry most of the polish.
- Use subtle texture, borders, or glow only where it adds depth.
- Make sections feel structured, not boxed-in.

## Visual Do Not

- Do not imitate terminal screens.
- Do not lean on bright green hacker tropes.
- Do not flood the UI with gradients, glass, or excessive motion.
- Do not make every card compete for attention.

## Experience Goal

The viewer should feel: This person is thoughtful, credible, and technically serious.
