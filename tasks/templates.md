# Task Templates

## Redesign Hero

### Goal
Improve first-screen clarity, trust, and premium feel without changing the factual profile.

### Required Inputs
- Current hero content
- docs/vision.md
- docs/brand-strategy.md
- docs/ui-system.md

### Constraints
- Keep role and transition truthful.
- Remove generic portfolio phrasing.
- Optimize for 10-second recruiter scan.

### Output Format
- Recommended headline
- Supporting line
- Trust signals to surface
- CTA strategy
- UX rationale
